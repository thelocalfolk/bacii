Order Processing

We aim to dispatch all orders within 1–3 business days from our Sydney warehouse.

During busy periods (such as launches or sales), dispatch times may be slightly longer — we'll always do our best to get your order out as quickly as possible.

Shipping (Australia)

We ship all orders via Australia Post.

  • Standard shipping: $12 AUD
  • Free shipping on orders over $100 AUD

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking details.

For overseas orders please contact us at hello@baciithelabel.com

Tracking Your Order

To track your order, please visit: https://www.auspost.com.au/track/

Please allow up to 24 hours for tracking to update after dispatch.
