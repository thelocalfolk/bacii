At Bacii, we believe little clothes should be made with big care.

When creating Bacii, we spent months searching for manufacturing partners who shared our commitment to quality, responsible production and thoughtful craftsmanship.

Today, we work with two specialist factories in China that produce our clothing and headwear. Both factories operate under internationally recognised standards and have experience producing high-quality products for brands around the world.

Many of our fabrics are made from certified organic cotton and produced in facilities that follow recognised environmental and social compliance practices, including GOTS, OEKO-TEX and BSCI standards.

While we're a small brand, we're committed to asking questions, choosing partners carefully and continually improving as we grow.

Because childhood should be messy, magical and full of personality - and the clothes that come along for the ride should be made with care
