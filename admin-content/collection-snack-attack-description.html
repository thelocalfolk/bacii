Snack Attack is the first collection from Bacii — a playful nod to one of childhood's greatest obsessions: snacks. Featuring hand-illustrated characters, nostalgic colours and personality-packed slogans, the collection celebrates the everyday moments that make raising little ones so memorable. Designed to be comfortable, durable and handed down, each piece is crafted from premium organic cotton and made for kids who are busy exploring, playing and, of course, asking what's for snack next.
