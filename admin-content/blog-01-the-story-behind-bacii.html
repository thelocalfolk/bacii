Bacii began around our kitchen table, somewhere between school drop-offs, snack requests and the beautiful chaos of raising two little boys.

As a mum to, two beautiful boys I was constantly searching for children's clothing that felt playful, comfortable and high quality without being overly franchise driven or too serious. I wanted basics every day pieces with personality. Clothes that kids were excited to wear and parents loved to buy.

The name Bacii comes from my Italian heritage and the word for "kisses" – a small reminder of family, love and the little moments that make childhood so special.

Every Bacii piece is designed with real kids in mind. The ones who climb trees, jump in puddles, wear tomato sauce on their sleeves and somehow come home covered in sand.

We believe childhood should be messy, magical and full of personality.

Thank you for being here at the beginning of our journey.
