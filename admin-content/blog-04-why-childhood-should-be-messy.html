At Bacii, we don't believe childhood should be spent worrying about keeping clothes clean.

Some of the best memories are made with muddy knees, sticky fingers and grass stains.

Children learn through play. They explore, experiment, climb, build, create and occasionally cover themselves in things you'd rather not identify.

That's exactly how it should be.

We design our clothing to be worn, lived in and loved. Not saved for special occasions.

The worn elbows, faded prints and mystery stains are often signs of a childhood being fully enjoyed.

So if your little one comes home covered in dirt after a day of adventures, we think that's something worth celebrating.

After all, childhood is messy.

And that's where the magic happens.
